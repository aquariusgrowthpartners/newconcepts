Clarity First

Commonly Asked.

Transparency is the foundation of every successful project. Here are the answers to questions we hear most often.

01. What is the typical timeline for a kitchen remodel?

While every project is unique, a comprehensive kitchen renovation typically spans 8 to 12 weeks. This includes structural changes, material procurement, and final artisanal finishes.

02. Do you provide architectural design services?

Yes. Our approach is holistic. We house both designers and craftsmen under one roof, ensuring that the architectural integrity of your concept is maintained through to completion.

03. How are project quotes calculated?

Quotes are meticulously calculated based on material selection, structural complexity, and labor precision. We provide a transparent breakdown to ensure full alignment with your investment.

04. Are you licensed and insured in Minnesota?

Absolutely. New Concepts is fully licensed and insured for all residential remodeling work within the state of Minnesota, adhering to the highest safety and quality standards.
